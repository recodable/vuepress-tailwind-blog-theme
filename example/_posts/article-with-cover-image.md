# This is an article with a cover image

We can even include a credit section and a link to the person.

With a simple metadata on top of your markdown like so:

```yaml
title: Article with cover image
date: 2020-02-20
cover:
  url: /magic.jpg
  credit:
    name: Mervyn Chan on Unsplash
    url: https://unsplash.com
```
